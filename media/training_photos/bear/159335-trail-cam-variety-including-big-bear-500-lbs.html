    Trail cam variety including big bear - 500 lbs?

    No decent bucks lately but quite a variety the other night. Calendar is a day behind, time is correct


    The buck has broken rear leg - hence the weird rack , that's how he was in velvet. Don't like naming bucks much - but his nickname is "tripod".
    I passed him up last Saturday morning. He was enjoying his rutting activities and gave him an "A" for effort but probably should have put him down but he already made it through one winter. He moves pretty quick when he has to, although he did just miss getting hit by a car when I watched him cross the road.

    Anyway - quite a mixed bag, huge bear might push 500 lbs, the buck, the buck staring at the bear waiting his turn, a big coyote, and a coon...
    Only thing missing was a cougar...
